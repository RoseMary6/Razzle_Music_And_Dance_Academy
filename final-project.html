RAZZLE MUSIC AND DANCE ACADEMY

Established in 1999, Razzle Music and Dance Academy
is an International Dance Company celebrating dance and music in a unique way. Our aim to excel and offer
empowerment through integrity and hard work. Nurturing a positive and progressive environment for Dance
and Music education.

NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL PROJECTS

We are involved in various National and International Projects
all year round with schools, colleges and NGO's, individual artists and other institutions.
We have already compleated over 22 International Projects and have Won over 400+ Awards.

OUR VISION

To install a better understanding of International and Indian
dance and music forms of performing arts and help artists to explore,
create, experiment and learn in an environment of creative freedom ad
directional thinking. Facilitating training methods, collaboration
opportunities and create performances that inspire, connect and influence
a positive change in the society.

  • COMMUNITY EDUCATION

  • COMPANY REACH & NETWORKS
